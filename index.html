About

Otavio Zerbini is a 22-year-old Brazilian-born entrepreneur and former professional footballer. He is the founder of Creator OS — a program-model business helping creators and experts grow on YouTube and monetize through digital products.

Built around a high-performance daily routine of training, building, and learning, Otavio is shifting his business from outbound-driven to inbound and brand-led growth — on the way to building a category-defining personal brand for the next generation of online operators.

Early life

Otavio was born in Boston, USA to Brazilian parents but grew up in Cape Town, South Africa, playing for academies — long mornings, double sessions, and a quiet obsession with becoming the best version of himself. By his late teens he was competing at a senior level.

A turning point pushed him to channel that same intensity into business. The discipline carried over: training every morning, building every afternoon, learning every night. What began as content on YouTube and Instagram evolved into a long-term plan to build an education business.

Company

Creator OS

Founded in 2024, Creator OS is the program-model business that helps creators and experts grow on YouTube and monetize through digital products. The flagship offer is a high-touch program built around a single promise — turn an audience into an asset.

It is the operating system Otavio wishes existed when he started: frameworks, coaching, and a community of operators executing at the top. Creator OS exists to compress the years it takes to build a category-defining personal brand into a focused, repeatable system.
